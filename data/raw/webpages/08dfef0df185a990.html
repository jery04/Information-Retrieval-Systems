Best Chrome Extensions

Just clear explanations, practical guides, and real use cases that help you understand how extensions work – and how to use them without breaking your browser or exposing your data.

Chrome extensions can feel simple on the surface. You install one, click an icon, and it does its job. But behind that simplicity sits a lot of moving parts – permissions, scripts, background processes, network requests. Sometimes things go wrong. Sometimes they slow everything down. Sometimes they ask for more access than they should.

That’s where our website comes in.
