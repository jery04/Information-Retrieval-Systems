Frequently Asked Questions

Below are common questions our customers have. Of course, we’re happy to meet with you and answer these and other questions you might have.
What Vue versions does NES support?

NES for Vue supports version 2. We provide ongoing support and patches to keep your applications secure and operational.

Does NES for Vue help with compliance?

NES for Vue helps maintain compliance by providing security patches and updates, even for older versions. Additionally, our enterprise-grade SLA stands up to HIPAA, SOC-2, PCI DSS, etc., and ensures your compliance with these standards. Many CVEs may affect older Vue versions, and we address those for you.

Why do I need NES for Vue?

Using an unsupported version of Vue exposes your applications to security vulnerabilities and compliance risks. Upgrading to the latest version can be costly and time-consuming. Never-Ending Support (NES) for Vue allows you to stay on your current version and remain supported until you are ready to upgrade.

How does licensing work?

When you purchase Never-Ending Support, you acquire a commercial license for the support services. Our pricing model is based on the number of users who will be committing code to the project repo. Users are unnamed and transferable across team members.

I got an error like "EOL/Obsolete Software: Vue 2 Detected." What can I do?

This means your application is running Vue 2, which has reached end-of-life and no longer receives official updates. Vue 2 NES provides ongoing security patches and compatibility fixes to keep your application running safely. Talk to our sales team to explore your options.

What version of Vue do I need to be on before switching to NES for Vue 2?

HeroDevs provides both 2.6.x and 2.7.x branches for NES for Vue 2, so you'll need to be on Vue 2.6.14 or 2.7.16, depending on which branch you plan to use.

Does HeroDevs have an SLA for NES for Vue 2?

Yes. Our turn-around times vary based on the severity of the issues found / reported. Security-related issues and data corruption issues are top priority and will be resolved immediately (same day if possible). If required we can provide our SLA for your team to review.

Where are the HeroDevs team members located?

HeroDevs is a distributed company with team members spread across North America, Central America, South America and Europe. However, we guarantee we’ll be available all hours of the day.

Which browsers does NES for Vue 2 offer support for?

NES for Vue 2 offers browser compatibility for Chrome, Firefox, Safari and Edge. This is the same assurance that the Vue team provides. 

Do we need to upgrade to Vue 2.7 before switching to NES for Vue 2?

Yes. Prior to moving to NES for Vue 2 your projects need to be running the latest version of Vue, which is currently 2.7.14.
