Type Lovers

Latinotype® is a renowned type foundry, based in Chile, that since 2010 has contributed to redefining global type design. With more than 15 years of experience, today it is considered one of the most influential type companies in the world. Its portfolio brings together more than 5,000 fonts in 300 families, used in advertising, branding, and digital design. Our most recognized typeface is Recoleta, used by major brands around the world.
