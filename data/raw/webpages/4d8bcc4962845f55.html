    Markdown is the Answer.
    The question is irrelevant...

    LLMs think in it. GEO runs on it. Your CMS should too. LLMs understand Markdown natively — it's the format they were trained on. Tina stores everything as Markdown in your Git repo, so your content stays clean, portable, and AI-friendly from the moment you publish.
